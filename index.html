The Science Behind

Understanding brain waves and neural patterns that power the Prometeo Tiara technology.

Brain Wave Analysis

Our technology analyzes different brain wave frequencies to understand cognitive states:

  • Alpha waves (8-12 Hz): Relaxed, meditative states
  • Beta waves (12-30 Hz): Active thinking, focus
  • Theta waves (4-8 Hz): Creativity, deep meditation
  • Delta waves (0.5-4 Hz): Deep sleep, healing

EEG Signal Processing

Advanced signal processing techniques clean and interpret EEG data:

  • Noise reduction algorithms for clean signals
  • Real-time Fourier transforms for frequency analysis
  • Machine learning classification of mental states
  • Pattern recognition for user-specific calibration
